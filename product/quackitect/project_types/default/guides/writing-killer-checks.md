## Guide (load on demand)
A killer check is a step that is dangerous to skip and easy to forget. Keep the gated set
short (≤9), word each as one checkable claim in the domain's own language, place it at a real
pause point, and revise it whenever it misses something. Killer checks are always human-adjudicated
gates — they cannot be tailored away, only reworded.
