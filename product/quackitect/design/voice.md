## Guide (load on demand)
This is the GENERIC engine voice. Replace it by placing your own `brand/voice.md` in your overlay.

Default rules: write plainly; one thought per sentence; short sentences; lists for three or more items;
do not repeat (single source of truth); lead with the point. Neutral, professional, no house personality.
